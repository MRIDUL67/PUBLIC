A wiki (/ˈwɪki/ ⓘ WICK-ee) is a form of hypertext publication on the
internet which is collaboratively edited and managed by its audience
directly through a web browser.A typical wiki contains multiple pages
that can either be edited by the public or limited to use within an
organization for maintaining its internal knowledge base. Its name
derives from the first user-editable website called "WikiWikiWeb",
with "wiki" being a Hawaiian word meaning "quick".[1]
